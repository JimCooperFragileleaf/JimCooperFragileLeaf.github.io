During September and October of 2016, a group of Sustainable Development masterâ€™s students at Utrecht University embarked on a project examining food waste at the University. Specifically, they were interested in the amount wasted at events and meetings organised at the University and what solutions could be put into place to reduce this. Their results showed that over a two-week period the amount of food and beverages which were thrown way after such occasions was the equivalent of 256 kg of CO2 and 160000 litres of water. To put this into context it was the same as driving was 1600 km in an average car and having 2000 baths. To maintain awareness of the issue, this website was created. Its aim is simple, to make you think about the impact your lunch has.
